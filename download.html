System Requirements

Minimum and recommended specifications for optimal performance

Windows Requirements

Minimum Requirements

OS: Windows 10 (1903) or later
RAM: 4 GB
Storage: 500 MB free space
Processor: x64 compatible

Recommended

OS: Windows 11
RAM: 8 GB or more
Storage: SSD with 1 GB free
Processor: Multi-core x64

macOS Requirements

Minimum Requirements

OS: macOS 11.0 (Big Sur)
RAM: 4 GB
Storage: 200 MB free space
Processor: Intel or Apple Silicon

Recommended

OS: macOS 13+ (Ventura)
RAM: 8 GB or more
Storage: SSD with 500 MB free
Processor: Apple M1/M2 or Intel i5+

RAM: 2 GB
Storage: 150 MB free space
Processor: x64 compatible

Recommended

OS: Ubuntu 22.04+ LTS
RAM: 4 GB or more
