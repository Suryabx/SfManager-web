Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Satan Encryptor

Is Satan Encryptor really secure?

Yes, Satan Encryptor uses military-grade AES-256 encryption, the same standard used by governments and financial institutions worldwide. Your data is completely secure.

Can I recover my files if I forget the password?

No, we use zero-knowledge encryption, which means we cannot recover your password or decrypt your files. Make sure to store your passwords securely.

Is Satan Encryptor free to use?

Yes, Satan Encryptor is completely free and open source. You can use all features without any limitations or hidden costs.

Which operating systems are supported?

Currently, we support Windows. macOS and Linux versions are coming soon. All versions will have the same features and compatibility.
